Our Mission

Halal Lyfestyle LLC is committed to uplifting communities through programs that reflect the values of inclusion, sustainability, creativity, and opportunity. Through our various initiatives, we demonstrate that every life—regardless of race, age, or gender—deserves dignity and empowerment.

About Built Lives Matter

Built Lives Matter is a campaign and outreach program that supports underserved communities by connecting them with vital resources like community solar energy, assisted living support, and job placement opportunities. Our mission is to recognize the potential in every individual and to build lives that thrive through awareness, action, and access.

Creative Minds Campaign

We proudly recognize our Creative Minds Campaign, which honors talent in music, art, innovation, and design. This platform allows individuals to showcase their gifts and gain recognition. Whether you're a musician, painter, coder, or poet — we want to see what you're building!

Interested in contributing your talents or joining one of our teams? Visit the Join Us page to inquire, apply, or volunteer.
